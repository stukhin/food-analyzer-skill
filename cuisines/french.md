# French

## Profile summary

French cuisine has a strong **butter + cream + cheese baseline** (gratin, béchamel, meunière, beurre blanc, gratinée, foie gras, soufflé, mousse) and a separate **light raw / grilled / steamed seafood vein** (tartare, plateau de fruits de mer, bouillabaisse, grilled fish + sauce on side, soupe de poisson without rouille).

The redeeming culinary lever: **"sauce on the side"** is well-understood and almost always honored — most pan sauces, vinaigrettes, drizzles can be served séparée. The structural cream/butter dishes (carbonara-equivalents in French — gratin, soufflé, ratatouille with cream) are not modifiable.

## Standard dishes

### Tartare de bœuf (beef tartare)
- **Components:** raw lean beef (tenderloin / sirloin) finely chopped + capers + cornichons + shallots + Dijon + Worcestershire + raw egg yolk + olive oil
- **Macros (one entrée ~180g beef):**
  - Calories: 380–500 kcal
  - Fat: 22–32g (sat 7–10g; mostly from yolk + oil)
  - Carbs: 4–8g
  - Protein: 36–46g
- **Modifications possible:**
  - "no egg yolk" — sometimes
  - "less oil" — sometimes
- **Heuristics:** lean cut (tenderloin); raw means no added cooking fat
- **Confidence:** HIGH

### Tartare de saumon / thon (fish tartare)
- **Components:** raw lean fish + citrus + olive oil + herbs + shallots
- **Macros (one entrée ~150g fish):**
  - Calories: 280–380 kcal
  - Fat: 18–26g (sat 3–4g — fish + oil)
  - Carbs: 2–6g
  - Protein: 28–36g
- **Heuristics:** omega-3 bonus if salmon; lean fat profile
- **Confidence:** HIGH

### Steak frites
- **Components:** grilled or pan-seared steak (entrecôte / faux-filet / onglet) + french fries + sauce (béarnaise / poivre / bordelaise)
- **Macros (one entrée — 200g steak + 150g frites + sauce):**
  - Calories: 780–1080 kcal
  - Fat: 42–62g (sat 14–22g — steak + frites + sauce)
  - Carbs: 50–65g
  - Protein: 42–58g
  - Sodium: 900–1500mg
- **Modifications possible:**
  - "sauce on side" — always (always honored)
  - "salad instead of frites" — almost always
  - "leaner cut" (filet vs entrecôte) — always (different menu item)
- **Heuristics:** cut discrimination matters a lot; the sauce + frites are the swing factors
- **Confidence:** HIGH

### Bouillabaisse
- **Components:** Provençal fish stew — multiple lean fish (rouget, daurade, conger eel, etc.) + tomato + saffron + fennel + garlic + olive oil + rouille (mayo + garlic + saffron) on side
- **Macros (one bowl + 1 toast + rouille):**
  - Calories: 480–660 kcal
  - Fat: 22–34g (sat 4–7g; rouille adds saturated component)
  - Carbs: 30–45g
  - Protein: 35–48g
- **Modifications possible:**
  - "no rouille" — always (cuts 100–150 kcal + 12–18g fat)
  - "less bread" — always
- **Heuristics:** broth fork (clear, fish-based — light end); rouille is the swing factor
- **Confidence:** MEDIUM (regional variation)

### Confit de canard (duck leg)
- **Components:** duck leg slow-cooked in its own fat, served crispy with side (often pommes sarladaises — potatoes cooked in duck fat)
- **Macros (one leg + potato side ~350g):**
  - Calories: 680–880 kcal
  - Fat: 48–66g (sat 16–22g)
  - Carbs: 30–42g
  - Protein: 38–48g
- **Modifications possible:**
  - "lighter side (salad / steamed veg)" — always
  - "skin removed" — sometimes (drops fat ~10g)
- **Heuristics:** structural saturated fat
- **Confidence:** HIGH

### Coq au vin
- **Components:** chicken (often thigh) braised in red wine + bacon + mushrooms + pearl onions + butter
- **Macros (one portion ~350g):**
  - Calories: 480–640 kcal
  - Fat: 26–38g (sat 9–13g; bacon + butter + chicken skin)
  - Carbs: 12–20g
  - Protein: 38–48g
  - Sodium: 800–1200mg
- **Modifications possible:**
  - "no bacon lardons" — sometimes
  - "skinless chicken" — sometimes
- **Heuristics:** cut + bacon double load
- **Confidence:** HIGH

### Foie gras (au torchon / poêlé)
- **Components:** fattened duck/goose liver
- **Macros (one slice ~50g):**
  - Calories: 220–280 kcal
  - Fat: 22–28g (sat 8–10g)
  - Protein: 6–8g
  - Sodium: 250–400mg
- **Modifications possible:**
  - "small portion" — always
- **Heuristics:** concentrated saturated fat in small package; cholesterol very high
- **Confidence:** HIGH

### Quiche lorraine
- **Components:** pastry crust + eggs + cream + lardons (bacon) + Gruyère
- **Macros (one slice ~180g):**
  - Calories: 480–620 kcal
  - Fat: 32–46g (sat 16–22g)
  - Carbs: 28–38g
  - Protein: 16–22g
- **Modifications possible:** none structural
- **Heuristics:** cream + cheese + bacon + butter pastry stack
- **Confidence:** HIGH

### Ratatouille
- **Components:** stewed eggplant + zucchini + bell pepper + tomato + onion + garlic + olive oil + herbs (no cream, no cheese in classic version)
- **Macros (one bowl ~300g):**
  - Calories: 180–260 kcal
  - Fat: 8–14g (sat 1–2g — olive oil)
  - Carbs: 22–32g (sugar 12–18g from vegetables)
  - Protein: 4–6g
  - Fiber: 7–10g
- **Modifications possible:**
  - "less oil" — sometimes
- **Heuristics:** quality fat; vegetable-dense; fiber bonus
- **Confidence:** HIGH

### Croque-monsieur / croque-madame
- **Components:** ham + Gruyère + béchamel + buttered bread, grilled (madame adds fried egg on top)
- **Macros (one croque ~200g):**
  - Calories: 540–740 kcal
  - Fat: 32–46g (sat 16–22g)
  - Carbs: 35–48g
  - Protein: 24–32g
  - Sodium: 1100–1600mg
- **Modifications possible:** none structural (cheese melt is the dish)
- **Heuristics:** cheese melt + béchamel + butter triple load
- **Confidence:** HIGH

### Niçoise salad
- **Components:** tuna + boiled egg + green beans + potato + tomato + olives + anchovies + vinaigrette
- **Macros (one entrée ~400g):**
  - Calories: 420–560 kcal
  - Fat: 22–32g (sat 4–6g; mostly olive oil + olives)
  - Carbs: 20–30g
  - Protein: 32–42g
  - Sodium: 900–1400mg (olives + anchovies)
- **Modifications possible:**
  - "dressing on side" — always
  - "no anchovies" — always (cuts sodium ~300mg)
- **Heuristics:** quality fat; protein-dense; salty sides
- **Confidence:** HIGH

### Crème brûlée
- **Components:** cream + egg yolks + sugar + vanilla, caramelized sugar top
- **Macros (one ramekin ~150g):**
  - Calories: 380–500 kcal
  - Fat: 26–38g (sat 16–22g)
  - Carbs: 22–32g (sugar 18–26g)
  - Protein: 4–6g
- **Modifications possible:** none
- **Heuristics:** indulgence framework candidate
- **Confidence:** HIGH

### Soupe à l'oignon gratinée
- **Components:** caramelized onion broth + crouton + Gruyère melted on top
- **Macros (one bowl ~350g):**
  - Calories: 380–500 kcal
  - Fat: 20–28g (sat 12–17g — cheese cap)
  - Carbs: 32–42g (sugar 8–12g from caramelized onion)
  - Protein: 16–22g
  - Sodium: 1300–1900mg
- **Modifications possible:**
  - "less cheese on top" — sometimes
  - "no cheese cap" — rare but possible
- **Heuristics:** sodium watch
- **Confidence:** HIGH

### Sole meunière (pan-fried fish in butter)
- **Components:** sole or other white fish dredged in flour + pan-fried in butter + lemon
- **Macros (one fillet ~180g):**
  - Calories: 380–520 kcal
  - Fat: 24–36g (sat 12–18g — butter dominant)
  - Carbs: 4–8g
  - Protein: 32–42g
- **Modifications possible:**
  - "less butter" / "butter on side" — sometimes (changes preparation)
- **Heuristics:** lean fish but butter cooking = hidden fat
- **Confidence:** HIGH

### Cheese plate (assortment)
- **Components:** 3–5 cheeses (Brie, Camembert, Comté, Roquefort, chèvre)
- **Macros (one tasting plate ~100g total cheese):**
  - Calories: 380–500 kcal
  - Fat: 32–42g (sat 18–26g)
  - Carbs: 1–4g
  - Protein: 22–30g
  - Sodium: 700–1100mg
- **Modifications possible:**
  - "smaller plate / share" — always
- **Heuristics:** concentrated saturated fat
- **Confidence:** HIGH

## Common red flags in this cuisine

- **Cream / butter / meunière / gratin / béchamel** in name — hidden butter and cream
- **Foie gras** — concentrated saturated fat + cholesterol
- **Confit (duck, pork)** — cooked in own fat
- **Pâté / rillettes / terrine** — concentrated rendered fat
- **Marbled beef** (entrecôte, faux-filet) — fattier cut
- **Lamb in most preparations** — fattier protein
- **Offal — kidney, sweetbreads** — cholesterol high
- **Escargots (garlic butter)** — butter is the dish
- **Cheese platters / pâtés** — concentrated saturated fat
- **Soufflés** — butter + cream + cheese + egg structural
- **Desserts (crème brûlée, mousse, tarte tatin, profiteroles, éclair)** — cream + butter + sugar combinations
- **Sauces with butter mounting ("monter au beurre")** — invisible 1–2 tbsp butter swirled at finish

---

## Common safe choices

- **Tartare (beef lean cut or fish)** — raw, lean, light cooking fat
- **Plateau de fruits de mer / huîtres / crevettes** — raw oysters, shrimp, mussels — light, protein
- **Bouillabaisse without rouille** — fish broth, lean protein
- **Grilled fish + sauce on side** — meunière can be requested without butter (rare but possible)
- **Niçoise salad with dressing on side, no anchovy** — protein + vegetables
- **Ratatouille** — vegetable-dense, olive oil only
- **Lentils (salade de lentilles) without foie gras** — fiber + protein
- **Soupe de poisson (clear fish soup, no rouille)** — lean broth
