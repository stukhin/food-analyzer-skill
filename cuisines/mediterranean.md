# Mediterranean

## Profile summary

Mediterranean (Greek, Levantine, southern Italian, Spanish coastal, French Provençal) is widely cited as a baseline healthy pattern: olive oil + vegetables + legumes + fish + whole grains + small portions of meat + cheese in moderation. Cooking is usually grilled / baked / stewed rather than deep-fried. The traps: feta and halloumi can push saturated fat; phyllo-based dishes (baklava, spanakopita) carry hidden butter + sugar; moussaka and pastitsio use béchamel; falafel and many "vegetarian" mezze are deep-fried.

Olive oil drizzle at restaurants is quality fat but caloric — 1–3 tbsp adds 15–40g fat invisibly.

## Standard dishes

### Greek salad (horiatiki)
- **Components:** tomato, cucumber, onion, olives, feta, oregano, olive oil
- **Macros (one plate ~300g):**
  - Calories: 320–460 kcal
  - Fat: 22–34g (sat 7–11g — feta + olives)
  - Carbs: 12–18g (sugar 6–10g from tomato)
  - Protein: 10–16g
  - Fiber: 4–6g
  - Sodium: 700–1100mg (feta + olives)
- **Modifications possible:**
  - "less feta" — always
  - "less oil" — always
  - "olives on side" — sometimes
- **Heuristics:** quality fat (olive oil +) vs saturated (feta −); sodium watch
- **Confidence:** HIGH

### Hummus + pita
- **Components:** chickpea + tahini + lemon + garlic + olive oil + cumin; pita bread
- **Macros (4 tbsp hummus + 1 pita):**
  - Calories: 350–460 kcal
  - Fat: 16–22g (sat 2–3g — olive oil + tahini)
  - Carbs: 42–55g (sugar 2–4g)
  - Protein: 12–16g
  - Fiber: 7–10g
- **Modifications possible:**
  - "less oil on top" — always
  - "half pita" or "veggie sticks instead" — always
- **Heuristics:** legume + fiber bonus; quality fat
- **Confidence:** HIGH

### Grilled fish + vegetables (Mediterranean style)
- **Components:** white fish or salmon + olive oil + lemon + herbs; grilled vegetables (zucchini, eggplant, pepper)
- **Macros (one plate ~350g):**
  - Calories: 380–520 kcal
  - Fat: 18–28g (sat 3–5g; mostly olive oil + fish omega-3)
  - Carbs: 10–18g
  - Protein: 35–48g
  - Fiber: 5–8g
- **Modifications possible:**
  - "no extra oil drizzle" — always
- **Heuristics:** ideal Mediterranean plate; omega-3 bonus if fatty fish
- **Confidence:** HIGH

### Lentil soup (faki / mercimek)
- **Components:** lentils + onion + carrot + olive oil + cumin + lemon
- **Macros (one bowl ~350g):**
  - Calories: 220–300 kcal
  - Fat: 5–9g (sat 1g)
  - Carbs: 32–42g (sugar 4–6g)
  - Protein: 13–17g
  - Fiber: 10–14g (excellent)
- **Modifications possible:**
  - none structural
- **Heuristics:** fiber bonus; legume protein
- **Confidence:** HIGH

### Falafel
- **Components:** chickpea / fava bean + herbs + spices, deep-fried
- **Macros (4 pieces ~120g):**
  - Calories: 320–440 kcal
  - Fat: 18–28g (sat 2–4g — oil absorbed)
  - Carbs: 28–38g
  - Protein: 12–16g
  - Fiber: 6–9g
- **Modifications possible:**
  - "baked instead of fried" — sometimes (rare; some places offer)
  - "share / smaller portion" — always
- **Heuristics:** cooking ladder (deep-fry penalty); legume base is good but fried
- **Confidence:** HIGH

### Tabbouleh
- **Components:** parsley + bulgur + tomato + mint + lemon + olive oil + onion
- **Macros (one plate ~200g):**
  - Calories: 160–220 kcal
  - Fat: 7–11g (sat 1g)
  - Carbs: 20–28g
  - Protein: 4–6g
  - Fiber: 5–7g
- **Heuristics:** light, fiber, quality fat
- **Confidence:** HIGH

### Baba ganoush
- **Components:** roasted eggplant + tahini + lemon + garlic + olive oil
- **Macros (4 tbsp ~100g):**
  - Calories: 140–200 kcal
  - Fat: 12–18g (sat 2g — tahini)
  - Carbs: 6–10g
  - Protein: 3–5g
  - Fiber: 3–5g
- **Heuristics:** tahini brings fat; smaller portion or veggie dippers
- **Confidence:** HIGH

### Moussaka
- **Components:** layered eggplant + potato + minced lamb + béchamel + cheese on top, baked
- **Macros (one slice ~300g):**
  - Calories: 480–640 kcal
  - Fat: 30–44g (sat 14–22g) — lamb + béchamel + cheese
  - Carbs: 25–35g
  - Protein: 22–32g
  - Sodium: 800–1200mg
- **Modifications possible:**
  - none structural (béchamel + cheese cooked in)
  - "smaller slice / share" — always
- **Heuristics:** layered hidden fat; structurally similar to lasagne
- **Confidence:** HIGH

### Tzatziki
- **Components:** Greek yogurt (strained) + cucumber + garlic + dill + olive oil
- **Macros (4 tbsp ~100g):**
  - Calories: 80–130 kcal
  - Fat: 5–9g (sat 2–4g — yogurt + olive oil)
  - Carbs: 4–7g
  - Protein: 5–8g
- **Heuristics:** light dip; protein
- **Confidence:** HIGH

### Spanakopita
- **Components:** spinach + feta + onion + herbs + olive oil, wrapped in phyllo (with butter brushed between layers), baked
- **Macros (one triangle ~120g):**
  - Calories: 280–380 kcal
  - Fat: 18–26g (sat 8–13g — feta + butter)
  - Carbs: 22–32g
  - Protein: 8–12g
  - Sodium: 600–900mg
- **Modifications possible:** none structural
- **Heuristics:** phyllo + butter = hidden fat; feta sodium
- **Confidence:** HIGH

### Octopus salad / grilled octopus
- **Components:** boiled then grilled octopus + olive oil + lemon + parsley + capers + red onion
- **Macros (one plate ~200g octopus):**
  - Calories: 220–320 kcal
  - Fat: 10–16g (sat 2g)
  - Carbs: 6–10g
  - Protein: 32–42g
- **Heuristics:** lean seafood; quality fat
- **Confidence:** HIGH

### Olive oil + bread (restaurant starter)
- **Components:** bread + dipping olive oil + balsamic
- **Macros (1 slice + 1 tbsp oil):**
  - Calories: 200–260 kcal
  - Fat: 14–16g (sat 2g — olive oil)
  - Carbs: 18–24g
  - Protein: 3–4g
- **Heuristics:** caloric but quality fat; portion control matters more than substance
- **Confidence:** HIGH

### Paella (mixed seafood + chicken)
- **Components:** rice + saffron + seafood (mussels, shrimp, calamari) + chicken + chorizo + bell pepper + olive oil
- **Macros (one plate ~400g):**
  - Calories: 520–700 kcal
  - Fat: 18–28g (sat 5–9g; chorizo bumps sat fat)
  - Carbs: 60–80g
  - Protein: 28–38g
  - Sodium: 1100–1700mg
- **Modifications possible:**
  - "no chorizo" — always (drops saturated fat ~6g)
- **Heuristics:** chorizo is the swing factor
- **Confidence:** MEDIUM (huge regional variation)

## Common red flags in this cuisine

- **Feta and halloumi** — saturated fat + high sodium; small portions are fine, restaurant portions add up
- **Phyllo-based dishes** (spanakopita, tiropita, baklava) — butter between layers is invisible
- **Béchamel-based dishes** (moussaka, pastitsio) — butter + cream + flour layer
- **Chorizo / sausages in paella** — saturated fat injection
- **Baklava + sweet pastries** — phyllo + butter + honey/sugar syrup + nuts
- **Restaurant olive oil drizzles** — quality fat but invisible 15–40g
- **Fried vegetable mezze** (zucchini chips, eggplant chips)

---

## Common safe choices

- **Grilled fish + vegetables** — Mediterranean baseline
- **Lentil soup, fasolada (bean soup)** — fiber + plant protein
- **Tabbouleh, fattoush, Greek salad with less feta** — fiber + vegetables
- **Hummus + veggie sticks** instead of pita-heavy version
- **Grilled octopus / shrimp / squid + lemon** — lean seafood
- **Roasted vegetables (briam, ratatouille)** — quality fat from olive oil only
