# American (incl. fast food + casual dining)

## Profile summary

American casual + fast food is dominated by **large portions**, **deep-fried sides** (fries, chicken), **cheese melt + bacon stacking** (burgers, sandwiches, "loaded" anything), and **sweetened beverages**. The cuisine is also the home of many **healthy-sounding traps** — "wellness bowls", "Caesar / Cobb salads" loaded with bacon and cheese, "fitness wraps" with mayo dressings.

The general lever: build à la carte (single patty + non-fried side + water), not combo (double patty + large fries + soda).

---

## Standard dishes

### Single-patty cheeseburger (small)
- **Components:** beef patty 100g, bun, cheese slice, lettuce, tomato, pickle, ketchup/mustard
- **Macros (one burger ~180g):**
  - Calories: 380–480 kcal
  - Fat: 18–28g (sat 8–12g)
  - Carbs: 30–40g (sugar 6–10g — bun + ketchup)
  - Protein: 22–28g
  - Sodium: 700–1100mg
- **Modifications possible:**
  - "no cheese" — always
  - "no mayo / no special sauce" — always
  - "lettuce wrap instead of bun" — sometimes
- **Heuristics:** structural — single patty + minimal extras is the workable version
- **Confidence:** HIGH

### Double-patty burger (Big Mac / Whopper class)
- **Components:** two beef patties + multiple cheese slices + special sauce (mayo-based) + bacon optional + bun
- **Macros (one burger ~300g):**
  - Calories: 720–960 kcal
  - Fat: 42–58g (sat 16–22g)
  - Carbs: 40–55g (sugar 9–14g)
  - Protein: 36–48g
  - Sodium: 1300–1900mg
- **Modifications possible:**
  - "no bacon" — always
  - "no special sauce" — always
  - "single patty version" — always (different menu item)
- **Heuristics:** large portion + stacked cheese + mayo trifecta
- **Confidence:** HIGH

### French fries (medium)
- **Components:** potato strips deep-fried in seed oil + salt
- **Macros (medium portion ~120g):**
  - Calories: 320–420 kcal
  - Fat: 15–22g (sat 2–4g)
  - Carbs: 42–55g
  - Protein: 4–6g
  - Sodium: 200–400mg
- **Modifications possible:**
  - "no salt added" — sometimes
  - "small instead of large/medium" — always
  - "side salad / fruit instead" — always
- **Heuristics:** cooking ladder (deep-fry penalty)
- **Confidence:** HIGH

### Fried chicken sandwich (Chick-fil-A / Popeyes class)
- **Components:** fried chicken fillet + bun + pickle + sauce (often mayo-based)
- **Macros (one sandwich ~250g):**
  - Calories: 500–700 kcal
  - Fat: 22–34g (sat 5–7g)
  - Carbs: 45–60g
  - Protein: 28–38g
  - Sodium: 1100–1700mg
- **Modifications possible:**
  - "no sauce" — always
  - "grilled instead of fried" — sometimes (Chick-fil-A and some chains offer)
- **Heuristics:** deep-fry penalty; sauce mayo-based
- **Confidence:** HIGH

### Grilled chicken sandwich
- **Components:** grilled chicken breast + bun + lettuce + tomato + light sauce
- **Macros:**
  - Calories: 380–500 kcal
  - Fat: 8–14g (sat 2–3g)
  - Carbs: 38–50g
  - Protein: 32–42g
  - Sodium: 900–1300mg
- **Modifications possible:**
  - "no mayo / sauce on side" — always
- **Heuristics:** the workable alternative to fried chicken sandwich
- **Confidence:** HIGH

### Caesar salad (with chicken)
- **Components:** romaine + grilled chicken + parmesan + croutons + bacon (often) + Caesar dressing (mayo + parmesan + anchovy + lemon)
- **Macros (one entrée salad ~350g):**
  - Calories: 480–680 kcal
  - Fat: 32–48g (sat 8–13g)
  - Carbs: 14–22g (sugar 3–5g)
  - Protein: 30–42g
  - Sodium: 1000–1500mg
- **Modifications possible:**
  - "dressing on side" — always (the biggest lever — cuts fat 30–50%)
  - "no bacon" — always
  - "no croutons" — always
  - "no parmesan" — sometimes
- **Heuristics:** **canonical healthy-sounding trap**; lever stack works well here
- **Confidence:** HIGH

### Cobb salad
- **Components:** lettuce + chicken + bacon + blue cheese + avocado + egg + tomato + dressing
- **Macros (one entrée ~400g):**
  - Calories: 580–780 kcal
  - Fat: 38–54g (sat 12–18g)
  - Carbs: 12–20g
  - Protein: 35–48g
  - Sodium: 1200–1800mg
- **Modifications possible:**
  - "dressing on side" — always
  - "no bacon" — always
  - "no blue cheese" — always
- **Heuristics:** healthy-sounding trap (multiple stacked fats)
- **Confidence:** HIGH

### Buffalo wings (fried)
- **Components:** chicken wings deep-fried + Buffalo sauce (cayenne + butter)
- **Macros (10 wings ~250g):**
  - Calories: 720–960 kcal
  - Fat: 48–66g (sat 14–20g)
  - Protein: 50–66g
  - Sodium: 1500–2300mg
- **Modifications possible:**
  - "grilled instead of fried" — sometimes
  - "sauce on side" — always
  - "smaller portion (6 instead of 10)" — always
- **Heuristics:** deep-fry + sauce double load
- **Confidence:** HIGH

### Mac & cheese
- **Components:** macaroni + cheese sauce (butter + flour + cheddar + milk/cream)
- **Macros (one bowl ~300g):**
  - Calories: 480–660 kcal
  - Fat: 24–36g (sat 14–22g)
  - Carbs: 45–60g
  - Protein: 16–22g
  - Sodium: 800–1200mg
- **Modifications possible:**
  - none structural (cheese is the dish)
  - "share / smaller portion" — always
- **Confidence:** HIGH

### Pancakes / waffles + syrup + butter
- **Components:** pancake batter + butter + maple syrup
- **Macros (3 pancakes + syrup + 1 tbsp butter):**
  - Calories: 560–740 kcal
  - Fat: 18–28g (sat 8–13g)
  - Carbs: 85–110g (sugar 35–55g — syrup is the dominant sugar)
  - Protein: 12–18g
- **Modifications possible:**
  - "syrup on side / less syrup" — always
  - "no butter" — always
  - "smaller stack (2 instead of 3)" — always
- **Heuristics:** sugar spike + saturated fat combo
- **Confidence:** HIGH

### Avocado toast
- **Components:** sourdough toast + smashed avocado + olive oil + salt + chili flakes + optional poached egg
- **Macros (with egg, one slice ~180g):**
  - Calories: 360–460 kcal
  - Fat: 22–30g (sat 4–6g; mostly avocado + olive oil)
  - Carbs: 28–38g
  - Protein: 12–18g
  - Fiber: 7–10g
- **Modifications possible:**
  - "no extra oil drizzle" — always
- **Heuristics:** quality fat; fiber bonus
- **Confidence:** HIGH

### BLT (bacon-lettuce-tomato sandwich)
- **Components:** bacon + lettuce + tomato + mayo + bread
- **Macros (one sandwich ~200g):**
  - Calories: 480–620 kcal
  - Fat: 28–40g (sat 8–12g)
  - Carbs: 28–38g
  - Protein: 18–24g
  - Sodium: 1100–1600mg
- **Modifications possible:**
  - "less bacon" / "fewer strips" — always
  - "mustard instead of mayo" — always
  - "open-face" — always
- **Heuristics:** bacon + mayo double saturated/processed fat
- **Confidence:** HIGH

### "Wellness bowl" / "protein bowl" (variable, café class)
- **Components:** typically grains (quinoa / rice) + protein (often grilled chicken or poke-style fish) + vegetables + "special sauce" + crunchy toppings (tempura crisps / fried garlic / candied nuts)
- **Macros (variable, often 600–900 kcal, fat 25–45g)**
- **Modifications possible:**
  - "sauce on side" — always
  - "no crunchy / tempura toppings" — always
  - "no extra dressing" — always
- **Heuristics:** **canonical healthy-sounding trap**; almost always has 2+ hidden-fat sources
- **Confidence:** MEDIUM (huge variation)

### Pizza by the slice (NY style, cheese)
- **Components:** dough + tomato sauce + mozzarella + olive oil drizzle
- **Macros (one slice ~150g):**
  - Calories: 320–420 kcal
  - Fat: 12–18g (sat 5–8g)
  - Carbs: 38–48g
  - Protein: 14–18g
  - Sodium: 700–1000mg
- **Confidence:** HIGH

### Milkshake (medium)
- **Components:** ice cream + milk + syrup; sometimes whipped cream
- **Macros (medium ~400ml):**
  - Calories: 580–780 kcal
  - Fat: 22–34g (sat 14–22g)
  - Carbs: 80–110g (sugar 65–90g)
  - Protein: 12–18g
- **Modifications possible:**
  - "small instead of medium/large" — always
  - "share" — always
- **Heuristics:** indulgence framework candidate; sugar + saturated fat dessert combo in a drink
- **Confidence:** HIGH

## Common red flags in this cuisine

- **Combo meals** — they bundle the worst version of each component (large fries, soda, large patty)
- **"Loaded" anything** (loaded fries, loaded nachos, loaded baked potato) — cheese + bacon + sour cream stack
- **Healthy-sounding salads** (Caesar, Cobb, Southwest, taco salad) — often 600–800 kcal with 30g+ fat
- **Mayo-based dressings and "special sauces"** — invisible 15–25g fat per serving
- **Sweetened sodas in large portions** — 250–400 kcal of pure sugar
- **Breakfast pastries / muffins from coffee chains** — 400–600 kcal, sugar + fat
- **Buffalo / BBQ / wing sauces** — fat or sugar load on top of already-fried protein

---

## Common safe choices

- **Grilled chicken sandwich + side salad** (no creamy dressing)
- **Single small burger + side salad + water**
- **Plain grilled fish + vegetables** (if available)
- **Black coffee, unsweetened tea, water, sparkling water**
- **Side salad with vinaigrette on side**
- **Avocado toast with simple egg** (café option)
- **Build-your-own bowl with grains + protein + vegetables + lemon-olive-oil instead of "special sauce"**
